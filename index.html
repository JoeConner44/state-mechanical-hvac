Our Services

Areas of Expertise

Our Specializations

State Mechanical brings deep expertise in three areas that many HVAC companies treat as secondary. For us, they are core to what we do — for both residential and commercial customers.

Mini-Split Systems

Ductless mini-split installation for additions, sunrooms, shops, server rooms, or any space where extending ductwork isn't practical. We size, source, and install the right system for your specific application.

Residential Commercial
Unit Change-Outs

When your system has reached the end of its life, a proper change-out makes all the difference. We handle the full process — removing old equipment, installing new matched systems, and ensuring everything is charged, tested, and running correctly before we leave.

Residential Commercial
Rooftop Units

Rooftop packaged unit installation, service, and replacement for commercial buildings, retail spaces, and flat-roof residential properties. We have the equipment and experience to work safely at height and get the job done right.

Commercial Residential

Service 03

System Cleanings & Tune-Ups

A well-maintained system runs more efficiently, lasts longer, and is far less likely to fail on the hottest or coldest day of the year. Schedule your seasonal tune-up today.

  • Full system inspection & cleaning
  • Filter replacement
  • Coil cleaning (evaporator & condenser)
  • Refrigerant level check
  • Blower motor & belt inspection
  • Safety control testing

Who We Are

Built on Trust. Proven by Time.

Since 1973, State Mechanical Heating & Air has been the HVAC company that Walton County residents and businesses call when it matters most. What started as a commitment to honest, quality work has grown into more than five decades of trusted service.

We believe that a heating and air company's reputation is everything. Ours is built on showing up on time, diagnosing problems accurately, and standing behind every job we complete — whether it's a quick repair or a full system installation.

We serve both residential and commercial customers throughout Walton County. From single-family homes to large commercial facilities, our technicians bring the same level of care and expertise to every job.

When you call State Mechanical, you're not calling a call center or a national franchise. You're calling a local company that has been part of this community for over half a century. That means something to us — and we intend to keep earning it.
