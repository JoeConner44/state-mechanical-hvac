AC Repair in Walton County, GA

When your air conditioner stops working on a hot Georgia day, you need a technician who can diagnose the problem fast and fix it right. State Mechanical Heating & Air has been repairing air conditioning systems in Walton County since 1973 — we know this climate, and we know what breaks.

Common AC Problems We Fix

From refrigerant leaks and frozen coils to failed capacitors, bad contactors, and compressor issues, our technicians have seen it all. We carry common parts on our trucks so we can complete most repairs in a single visit.

We Work on All Makes and Models

Whether your system is American Standard, Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Goodman, or any other brand, we have the experience and tools to diagnose and repair it correctly. Residential and commercial systems both.

What’s Included

  • Full system diagnosis
  • Refrigerant leak detection & recharge
  • Capacitor & contactor replacement
  • Compressor diagnosis
  • Thermostat repair & replacement
  • Blower motor service
  • Evaporator & condenser coil service
  • Electrical component repair
