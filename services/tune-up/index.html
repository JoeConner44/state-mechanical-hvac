HVAC Tune-Up & Cleaning in Walton County, GA

A well-maintained HVAC system runs more efficiently, costs less to operate, and is far less likely to fail on the hottest or coldest day of the year. State Mechanical offers thorough seasonal tune-ups and system cleanings for homes throughout Walton County.

What Happens During a Tune-Up

Our technicians inspect and clean every major component of your system — from the coils and drain pan to the blower motor, electrical connections, and refrigerant levels. We document what we find and let you know if anything needs attention.

Best Time to Schedule

Spring and fall are ideal — before cooling season and before heating season. Getting ahead of the rush means shorter wait times and peace of mind before the weather changes.

What’s Included

  • Full system inspection
  • Evaporator & condenser coil cleaning
  • Air filter replacement
  • Drain line cleaning & treatment
  • Refrigerant level check
  • Electrical connection tightening
  • Blower motor & belt inspection
  • Thermostat calibration check
  • Safety control testing
