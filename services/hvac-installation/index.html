HVAC Installation in Walton County, GA

A properly installed HVAC system runs more efficiently, lasts longer, and keeps your home comfortable year-round. As an authorized American Standard dealer, State Mechanical sources and installs quality equipment — and we size every system correctly for your specific home.

Proper Sizing Makes All the Difference

An oversized system short cycles, wears out faster, and leaves your home feeling humid. An undersized one runs constantly and never catches up. We perform load calculations to make sure the equipment we install is right for your space.

Full Installation From Start to Finish

We handle everything — equipment selection, removal of old systems, installation, refrigerant charging, testing, and a full walkthrough so you understand your new system before we leave.

What’s Included

  • Central AC & heating system installation
  • Heat pump installation
  • Air handler installation
  • Ductwork modification & connection
  • Thermostat installation & programming
  • Load calculation & equipment sizing
  • Old equipment removal & disposal
  • Post-install inspection & walkthrough
