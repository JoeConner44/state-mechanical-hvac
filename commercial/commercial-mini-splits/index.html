Commercial Mini-Split Systems in Walton County, GA

Ductless mini-split systems solve some of the toughest HVAC challenges in commercial buildings — conditioning spaces where ductwork isn't practical, creating independent climate zones, and providing efficient spot cooling or heating exactly where it's needed.

Common Commercial Applications

Server rooms, restaurant back-of-house areas, retail additions, office build-outs, storage areas with temperature requirements, and any space that needs conditioning without a major ductwork project are all strong candidates for commercial mini-splits.

Multi-Zone Systems

Commercial mini-split systems can support multiple indoor units from a single outdoor unit, making them ideal for open floor plans, multi-room build-outs, and buildings where independent temperature control matters.

What’s Included

  • Single & multi-zone systems
  • Ceiling cassette, wall & floor units
  • High-capacity commercial equipment
  • Server room & critical environment cooling
  • Restaurant & food service applications
  • Retail & office build-outs
  • System design & load calculation
  • Full installation & commissioning
