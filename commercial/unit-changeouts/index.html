Commercial Unit Change-Outs in Walton County, GA

When a commercial HVAC system reaches the end of its useful life, a properly executed change-out makes all the difference. State Mechanical handles commercial unit change-outs from start to finish — removing old equipment, installing the right replacement, and verifying everything works correctly before we leave.

Getting the Replacement Right

A change-out isn't just swapping one unit for another. It's an opportunity to right-size the equipment, address any installation issues from the original system, and make sure the replacement is set up for maximum efficiency and longevity.

Minimal Downtime

We plan change-outs carefully to minimize disruption to your business. For critical operations, we coordinate scheduling and have contingency plans in place to keep downtime as short as possible.

What’s Included

  • Full equipment removal & disposal
  • Like-for-like & upgrade replacements
  • Proper refrigerant recovery & recharge
  • Electrical & controls connection
  • Ductwork modification if needed
  • Start-up testing & commissioning
  • Warranty registration
  • Documentation provided
