Our Story

52 Years of Trusted Service

Who We Are

Built on Trust. Proven by Time.

Since 1973, State Mechanical Heating & Air has been the HVAC company that Walton County residents and businesses call when it matters most. What started as a commitment to honest, quality work has grown into more than five decades of trusted service.

We believe that a heating and air company’s reputation is everything. Ours is built on showing up on time, diagnosing problems accurately, and standing behind every job we complete — whether it’s a quick repair or a full system installation.

We serve both residential and commercial customers throughout Walton County. From single-family homes to large commercial facilities, our technicians bring the same level of care and expertise to every job.

When you call State Mechanical, you’re not calling a call center or a national franchise. You’re calling a local company that has been part of this community for over half a century. That means something to us — and we intend to keep earning it.
